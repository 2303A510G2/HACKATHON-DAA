Crop Optimization

Optimal Crop Selection

Based on your soil test results and local weather patterns, we recommend the most suitable crops for maximum yield and profitability.

Your Recommended Crops
  • Primary Crop: Soybean (High yield potential: 3.2 tons/acre)
  • Secondary Crop: Chickpea (Good market demand)
  • Alternative: Pearl Millet (Drought resistant)

Planting Calendar

Crop Best Planting Window Harvest Period
Soybean June 15 - July 10 October - November
Chickpea October 1 - October 30 February - March
Pearl Millet July 1 - July 20 September - October

Cultivation Recommendations

  • Seed Rate: 60-70 kg/ha for soybean
  • Spacing: 45 cm between rows, 5 cm between plants
  • Fertilizer: 20:60:40 NPK per acre
  • Soil Preparation: 2 deep ploughings + 2 harrowings
